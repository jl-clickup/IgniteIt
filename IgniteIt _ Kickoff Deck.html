Why this matters now

Operating reality
  • Important work spans ClickUp, Airtable, Trello, ActiveCampaign, Canva, DocuSign, and other communication surfaces.
  • Manual handoffs create duplicate work and make it harder to see what is complete, what is blocked, and what still needs attention.
  • A lean team needs systems that keep pace as sponsor volume and event complexity increase.
Desired outcome
  • One clear intake path when a signed contract arrives.
  • Structured details captured once, then reused across downstream work.
  • Improved visibility into sponsor obligations, readiness, and follow-through before events go live.
