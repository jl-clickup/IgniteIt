04 · why this matters now
Current state

Why this matters now

IgniteIt is trying to support a lean team and growing event complexity without adding more fragmented tools, duplicate work, or manual handoffs.

Operating reality
  • Work is spread across ClickUp, Airtable, ActiveCampaign, Trello, Canva, Google Sheets, DocuSign, email, text, and event tooling.
  • Contract details and sponsor deliverables are re-entered manually, which increases mistakes and slows follow-through.
  • A lean team is supporting roughly six events per year, with readiness depending too heavily on memory and disconnected repositories.
Desired outcome
  • One clear intake path when a signed sponsorship contract arrives.
  • Structured details captured once and reused across downstream work.
  • Cleaner visibility into sponsor obligations, event readiness, and what is complete, outstanding, or at risk.
Scale

Lean team, heavy coordination load

Automation matters most where it removes repetitive interpretation and task setup.

Consistency

Repeat the right steps every time

Standardized contract-to-work logic reduces dropped handoffs and missed obligations.

Visibility

See readiness sooner

Better structure makes it easier for leadership and operators to identify blockers before events go live.
